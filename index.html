OUR FEET

It all starts with our feet.

Our feet go through a lot more than we think. It is important to take care of our feet from birth and throughout each phase of life. Neglected foot problems can spread infection to the other areas of the body and possibly the bloodstream.

It is important to make sure your foot healthcare or treatment is undertaken by a registered Practitioner whom you can trust to care for and treat your feet safely.

ABOUT US

We provide a mobile, caring and capable professional foot health care facility. Our aim is to keep you mobile, active and relieve pain in the comfort of your own home at your convenience.
